# Namaste :pray:


I'm Abhishek, an avid reader and a self-proclaimed language nerd, currently navigating my second year of master's studies at IIT Kanpur.

## Navigating 

My diverse interests in Physics, Programming, Space technology and Languages have found a harmonious convergence in the field of Geodesy. Geodesy, the art and science of understanding Earth's shape, gravity field, and rotation, is the perfect constellation where my diverse interests align.

## From Data to Discovery 

I want to build insights from geodetic data, transforming raw measurements into profound discoveries. The principles of Physics, modern computing and space technology enable decoding Earth's complex dynamics.

I invite you to join me on this thrilling journey. Explore my research, follow my academic pursuits, and read my blogs. Let's collaborate, learn, and advance together.
